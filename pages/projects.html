This image is from my final project in STAT 400: Data Mining and Machine Learning. It is a visualiztion of a neural network model we found when trying to predict stock prices. We used five companies, Google, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft, and Apple and the data from the last 5 years of their opening and closing prices. Although our model only had a 57% rate to predict the right price, I was still able to learn a lot of machine learning and neural networks.
